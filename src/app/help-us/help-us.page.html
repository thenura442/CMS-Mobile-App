Common Guidelines Steps and Guidelines

1. To Make Orders on the Application browse through items and add items to the cart.

2. In the Browse Page browsing any item required is possible.

3. After Selecting Items from a single store head to the cart and update it as you require.

4. Once cart is Updated checkout and confirm the order if the method of transaction preferred is cash.

5. If preferred payment method is card click on bank card and enter required details and click on Pay Now to Confirm the Order.

6. Once order is confirmed you will receive a confirmation message on a new screen and the order can be tracked on the Orders Page.

7. In the profile page updating profile and password can be carried out.

Reset Forgot Password

1. To reset the password if password is forgotten head to the forgot password tab from the login page and enter the email associated with us.

2. Once you receive the temporary password to your email, login to the account and change the password through the profile page.
